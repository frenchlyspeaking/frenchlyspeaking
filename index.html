About Me

Bonjour ! Moi, c'est Karina.

I'm a native French speaker from the South of France and dedicated language teacher with over 25 years of experience helping students navigate the learning pathway to the French language.

I started teaching in 2000 in Oxford, UK, at the Alliance Française and created Frenchly Speaking two years later.

I'm warm, encouraging, witty and kind but strict when it comes to the teaching, especially the pronunciation.

I love to share my love of the French language and France's quirky lifestyle. Come and join me ! :)

The Lessons

Passionate About Sharing The French Language

I work on all aspects of the language: vocabulary, communication, reading, writing and the most important in my view, pronunciation — a must that needs to be set right from the start or will set in wrong for ever — and grammar — as it is very hard to understand on your own and is essential if your plan is to master the language. Duolingo certainly won't help there! All this in the comfort of your own home or office.

One-to-One teaching allows me to best suit the needs and pace of my students — I'm here to guide you every step of the way. ;)
